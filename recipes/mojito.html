MOJITO

DESCRIPTION

This Mr.Gallochapaval's recipe for a classic mojito, with all the flavors of the Caribe

INGREDIENTS

  • 2oz - Zacapa 12 Rum or White Havana Rum
  • 1oz - Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 - Lemon
  • 2tbsp - Brown sugar
  • Club soda
  • Fresh mint
  • Ice

STEPS

  1. Place the Freshly squeezed lemon juice, the Brown sugar, and the Fresh mint inside a Highball glass.
  2. Using a Cocktail Muddler firmly press on the mixture, twisting a little to mash the ingredients together. (*NOTE: if you dont have a muddler, the back of a wooden spoon would do the job)
  3. Add the Ice all the way to the top!
  4. Add some Lemon Slices as well as some unmashed Mint Leaves as a garnish
  5. Pour the 2oz of Rum...hopefully you chose the Zacapa 12!!!
  6. Fill with the Club Soda and enjoy!
