MANDARINO

DESCRIPTION

This Mr.Gallochapaval's easy but refreshing tangerine cocktail

INGREDIENTS

  • 4.5oz - Vodka (My recommendation would be a Grey Goose Vodka for a milder flavor, or a Stolichnaya Vodka for a strong yet flavourful cocktail)
  • Tangerine Icecream
  • Ice

STEPS

  1. Place the Ice (around 6 big cubes) the Vodka and 2 scoops of the Tangerine Icecream in a blender.
  2. BLEND!
  3. Pour into a Hurricane or Goblet glass. Enjoy in the Sun!
